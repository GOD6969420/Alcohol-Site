Our Story

QuickQuench is a vibrant establishment nestled in the heart of Farringdon's Exmouth Market, offering a unique fusion of culinary experiences. As a multifaceted venue, QuickQuench proudly serves as a dynamic restaurant, inviting bar, and artisanal coffee roastery. Situated on a bustling corner site, the establishment boasts impressive floor-to-ceiling glass facades on two sides, providing patrons with sweeping views of the lively Exmouth Market and a vibrant London intersection. In addition to its exceptional culinary offerings, QuickQuench also offers an extensive selection of alcoholic beverages to complement your dining experience.
